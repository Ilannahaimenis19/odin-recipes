Chicken Breast Recipe

Description

This grilled chicken breast is juicy, flavorful, and easy to make. Perfect for a quick dinner or a meal prep option, it's seasoned with a smoky marinade and served with a zesty chimichurri sauce.

Whether you're grilling outdoors or using a grill pan at home, this recipe guarantees tender chicken with beautiful char marks and bold flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh parsley or chimichurri sauce (optional, for serving)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Coat the chicken breasts with the marinade and let sit for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the chicken for 5â€“7 minutes per side, or until cooked through and golden.
  5. Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  6. Serve with chimichurri sauce or fresh herbs.
