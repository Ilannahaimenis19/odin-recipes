Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna features layers of tender pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, and creamy béchamel, all topped with bubbling melted cheese.

It’s the ultimate comfort food, perfect for family dinners, leftovers, or freezing ahead for busy weeks.

Ingredients

  • 6 lasagna noodles
  • 300g ground beef
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles and set aside.
  2. Brown the ground beef, add tomato sauce, and season.
  3. Layer noodles, meat, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  4. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30 minutes.
  5. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
